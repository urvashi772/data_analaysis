**Shital Restaurant Dashboard**
**Project Overview**

The Shital Restaurant Dashboard is an end-to-end data analytics and visualization project built to analyze restaurant sales, user behavior, city-wise performance, and restaurant-level insights. The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders understand trends, identify high-performing cities and cuisines, and make data-driven decisions for growth, marketing, and operations.

This project demonstrates practical skills in data analysis, data modeling, and Power BI dashboard design, making it suitable for real-world business intelligence use cases.

**Objectives**

Analyze overall restaurant sales and order trends

Understand customer demographics and behavior

Identify top-performing cities, cuisines, and restaurants

Compare current year vs previous year sales

Provide actionable business insights and recommendations

Key Business Insights (Conclusion)
Sales & Revenue

Tirupati is the highest revenue-generating city with 43M in sales

Sales increased sharply by 356% in 2018, but declined by 17% in 2019

Vegetarian food generates 7.2% more revenue than non-vegetarian food

Customer Behavior

Customers aged 21–25 are the most active buyers

Students contribute the largest share of orders

Male customers generate higher revenue compared to female customers

Top 10% of customers contribute nearly 80% of total sales

City & Restaurant Performance

Bikaner leads in user count, rating count, and order volume

Raipur shows the strongest current-year sales growth

North Indian and Chinese cuisines dominate across cities
